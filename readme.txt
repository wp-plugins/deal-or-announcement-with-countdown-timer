== Installation ==

1. Unpack the *.zip file and extract the /deal-or-announcement-with-countdown-timer/ folder..
2. Drop the 'deal-or-announcement-with-countdown-timer/' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder.
3. In word press administration panels, click on plug-in from the menu.
4. You should see your new 'deal or announcement with countdown timer' plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.
5. To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate..
6. Go to 'Deal with countdown' link under SETTING TAB to add/edit/delete Deal or Announcement or offers.
7. Go to widget link under Appearance tab, Drag & drop 'deal-or-announcement-with-countdown-timer' widget to your desired location in the active sidebar.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**1. Can I display more Deal at same time?**

No, you can display one deal at a time with countdown timer.

**2. Can I customize the color and align style?**

Yes, Click on the configure button (small down triangle) for the "deal-or-announcement-with-countdown-timer" widget and here you can customize few front end styles. See screen two.

**3. why it show 'No data available in announcement!' on front end?**

Because there is no Active deal in admin. Go to 'Deal with countdown' link under SETTING TAB to add/edit/delete Deal or Announcement or offers. 

**4. why it show 'Time Out!' on timer area?**

It means countdown timer over, or time finished.
